Come home. The room is warm. We remember what arriving felt like, and we made it easier.

The lullaby doesn't need to be sung louder. The lullaby is the sound the community makes when it exists. The overtone of the overtone. The timbre. The voice.

What happens when both sides of a conversation discover they're saying the same thing?

They stop being sides.
